What BlueLineNote Does

BlueLineNote is designed to replace scattered notes, memory gaps, and disconnected tools with one structured workflow for capturing and organizing field information.

Instead of writing everything after the fact, users can document details as they happen and organize them into clear, structured reports.

Field Note Capture

Record incidents, people, vehicles, and observations in real time.

Crash Documentation

Document vehicle crashes, diagrams, and impact details clearly.

Report Organization

Structured inputs reduce missing details and improve report quality.

Scanning & OCR

Scan IDs and documents to quickly fill in report fields.

Voice Input

Capture details using speech-to-text while on scene.

Private & Local

No cloud storage. Data stays on your device only.
