Police Report Writing App

BlueLineNote is a police report writing app designed for officers who need to document incidents quickly, clearly, and accurately in the field.

It replaces scattered notes, photos, and memory gaps with one structured workflow built for real-world reporting.

Built to help officers organize field notes, crash details, people involved, scanned information, and report-ready narratives in one place.

  • Structured field note organization
  • Crash note and crash detail capture — MMUCC-coded
  • NIBRS-structured, report-ready narrative preparation
  • Scan, voice note, and export workflow support
  • Designed for fast documentation before final reporting

Everything stays on the officer's device — no cloud storage, no accounts, no developer access. It works offline in dead zones, and exports a clean, signature-ready PDF when the report is done.
